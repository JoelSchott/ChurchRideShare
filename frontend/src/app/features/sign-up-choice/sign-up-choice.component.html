Become a Rider

Riders are created by those who frequently request rides. You don't need to make an account to request a ride, but it makes the process easier. When you become a Rider, you aren't required to retype previously given information (name, address, phone number, etc.) any time you need a ride.

Become a Driver

Drivers are church members who will be providing the means of transportation. You can become a Driver by creating an !app! account and applying to your corresponding church. Upon approval, you will be able to view and accept ride requests.

Become a Church Admin

Church Admins are created by church staff. This allows the management of details on !app! such as service times, locations, and website links. This information is available to both Riders and Drivers. Church Admins also manage who can and can't participate as Drivers for their church.
